About Us
OnlyPics is a group project made for the University of Glasgow. The team hopes to create a great environment for talented people to be able to share their photographs with the whole world for other equally talented people to see or buy, whether that was landscape, portrait, fashion, or sports photography; our site is able to accommodate them.
